Rule of thumb going forward

Use:

showLocationIds when the location should appear face-down
revealLocationIds when it should flip face-up immediately
revealSpawnLocations when spawning an enemy should also reveal where it appears
engageOnSpawn when that spawned enemy should immediately engage if it appears at the investigator’s location

That gives you a nice scenario-authoring vocabulary without more store complexity.
